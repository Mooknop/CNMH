Father Ezakien Tobyn led Sandpoint's faithful before Abstalar Zantus, and died in the fire that consumed the town's original wooden chapel during the Late Unpleasantness. He raised the foundling Nualia as his foster daughter, and his public reputation was one of piety and discipline.

Those who remember him well speak more carefully. His strictness toward Nualia, some whisper, went beyond a guardian's firmness — and whatever the truth of it, the girl who grew up under his roof did not remember Sandpoint kindly.
