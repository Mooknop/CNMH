When the Sandpoint chapel burnt to the ground in 4702 AR, killing the town's priest, Ezakien Tobyn, his position in the town was filled by his top acolyte, Abstalar Zantus. Though Father Zantus is himself a cleric of Desna, his role as high priest of the since-rebuilt Sandpoint Cathedral requires him to be open to other faiths, and he is more than willing and open to assist followers of other gods with spiritual matters.
