Alma Avertin has run Sandpoint Savories for decades, and her pastries are as close to a civic institution as bread gets. She is the twin sister of Casp Avertin, the sheriff slain by Chopper — a grief she carries quietly under the warmth she shows every customer.

These days a fresher worry sits on top of the old one: her daughter Arika took up the adventuring life against Alma's wishes, and their last argument ended badly. Arika hasn't been home since.
