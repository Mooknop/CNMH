Casp Avertin was sheriff of Sandpoint during the Late Unpleasantness, and died as he lived: standing between the town and its dangers. He caught the killer called Chopper in the act and was murdered stopping him, becoming the spree's twenty-fifth and final victim. Belor Hemlock, then his deputy, avenged him and succeeded him as sheriff.

His twin sister Alma still bakes at Sandpoint Savories, and the Town Watch still tells his story to new recruits.
