Belimarius was the tenth and final Runelord of Envy, ruler of the Ancient Thassilonian realm of Edasseril. She reigned from -5402 AR until the fall of Thassilon in -5293 AR, when she and the inhabitants of her capital Xin-Edasseril were preserved outside of normal time rather than destroyed by Earthfall.

Very recently, adventurers freed Belimarius and the inhabitants of Xin-Edasseril from this state of captivity, and she has taken up the reins of leadership once again — now as co-ruler of the nation of New Thassilon alongside Runelord Sorshen. She wields Belimarius's Invidious Halberd, a weapon crafted by Xin himself for the previous Runelord of Envy.

The Runelord of Envy specializes in abjuration magic.
