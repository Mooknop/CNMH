Nualia was a foundling raised by Father Ezakien Tobyn at Sandpoint's old chapel. Her celestial heritage made her an object of fascination to the townsfolk — treated as a living icon by some and a curiosity by others, and shown precious little simple kindness by anyone. She was presumed dead in the fire of 4702 that destroyed the original chapel and killed Father Tobyn.

Years later she returned transformed: sworn to Lamashtu, her left arm twisted into a demonic talon, at the head of the plot that brought goblins down on Sandpoint. She was slain by the heroes of that era seventeen years ago and buried in a lonely corner of the Boneyard, set apart from the 'respectable' graves. Father Abstalar Zantus has long believed the town owes her an apology it never gave — and now that her stolen remains have been recovered from the catacombs, he means to deliver it by calling her spirit home.
