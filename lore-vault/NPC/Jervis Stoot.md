For years Jervis Stoot was simply Sandpoint's eccentric woodcarver, famed for the wooden birds he carved into posts, eaves, and doorframes — having a 'Stoot bird' on your home was a point of pride. He lived alone on the small isle off the northern cliffs, and no one thought twice about him.

Then the killings began. Over the course of a single terrible month, twenty-five townsfolk died to his hatchet, each found with eyes and tongue cut away. Sheriff Casp Avertin caught Stoot in the act and became his final victim before the killer was subdued and slain. Beneath Stoot's home, the town found a blood-soaked shrine to some winged demon; they burned the cottage, tore down the stairs carved into the cliff, and have shunned Chopper's Isle ever since.
