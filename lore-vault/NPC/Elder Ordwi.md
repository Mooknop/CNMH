Elder Ordwi is one of the governing council of Elders that leads Osprey Cove. She is a cleric of Cayden Cailean and a known friend of the natural world, embodying the town's values of freedom, community, and easy-going goodwill. She has become a personal friend of the party, and offered them hospitality — including lodging in the bar she runs — when they arrived in Osprey Cove.
