Hayliss runs her shop with the same faith her brother Jasper holds, and none of his diplomatic temperament. She charges Scarnetti family members extra and considers the mayor's requests to keep the peace to be advisory at best. She has recently seen something unusual involving the Vinder girl, and she's looking for someone discreet to investigate.
