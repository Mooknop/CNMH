The Red Bishop shares his true name with no one; it cannot be spoken aloud, only conveyed in an unsettling telepathic sending. He came to the Lost Coast in 4700 AR, drawn by his patron's influence over the region, and found two prizes: an ancient shrine to Pazuzu hidden in the rock of Chopper's Isle, and something far older in the depths of the Pit — a thin place between the Material Plane and Pazuzu's Abyssal realm of High M'vania, held shut by an ancient ward he cannot directly undo.

Preferring slow corruption to swift ruin, he has spent more than twenty years arranging Sandpoint's tragedies: it was the Red Bishop who steered Jervis Stoot to the statuette that made him Chopper, and the Red Bishop whose nightmare-whispers turned the town's heart against Nualia. Every doom Sandpoint has suffered has fed his design — and the seven worst are still to come.
