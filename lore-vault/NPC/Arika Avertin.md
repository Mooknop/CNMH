Arika Avertin grew up over the ovens at Sandpoint Savories and chose a sword over a rolling pin. Her first adventuring company plumbed the Pit and met a grisly end there — Arika claims they ran afoul of the Sandpoint Devil, and she alone came back.

Rather than give it up, she joined a new company and headed south toward Magnimar. The Runewatchers, who keep track of local adventuring groups, can confirm that much but little more. Her mother wants one message carried to her if anyone finds her: 'Mother is worried about you and wants you to come home.'
