Runelord Sorshen was the Runelord of Lust in Ancient Thassilon, ruler of the realm of Eurythnia. Her ancient capital was Xin-Eurythnia, now the site of the modern city of Korvosa. Like her peers, she fled into magical stasis when Earthfall threatened the empire.

In recent years, Sorshen awakened and took a strikingly different path from the other returning Runelords — choosing to work against destructive forces rather than seek conquest. She was instrumental in empowering adventurers in their fight against the returning Runelord Alaznist, and invented the Runewaking Ritual, a crucial piece of magic for those hoping to permanently destroy Runelord Zutha.

Sorshen has been collecting the weapons of her fallen fellow Runelords, storing them in her personal demiplane, the Eye of Desire, to protect the world from their power. She now rules a portion of New Thassilon.
