Akoni is a male dryad who makes his home in the Tickwood near Sandpoint, presiding over Akoni's Grove as lord of its fey court. His court is composed mainly of sprites and grigs, and he has long maintained a peaceful relationship with Sandpoint's hunters and nature-minded residents.
