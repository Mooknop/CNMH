Audrahni is a pale-skinned, dark-haired elf who dresses in somber colors and moves with an awkward gait and slumped posture rare among her kind. The awkwardness once extended to her personality, but her adventures alongside the heroes who faced Runelord Alaznist a few years ago restored her faith and left her confident and warm.

As a worshipper of Ashava — empyreal lord of dancers, lonely spirits, and moonlight — she is a gifted healer and offers her aid freely. She has come to Sandpoint at Abstalar's request to serve as primary caster of the Call Spirit ritual, for if any soul fits Ashava's definition of a lonely spirit in need of guidance, it is Nualia's.
