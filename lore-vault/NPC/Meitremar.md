Meitremar is a dangerous scholar who is picking up where his grandfather left off: attempting the resurrection of Xar-Azmak, the long-dead demon lord of rust who once ruled the Abyssal realm of Vlorus. He has found the research necessary for this project within the Thassilonian ruin of Rusthenge near Iron Harbor.

Meitremar is positioned as a member of Runelord Belimarius's government in Xin-Edasseril, giving his work the political backing and resources of New Thassilon. In practice this relationship is one of convenience — his sinspawn ally Azomi, while nominally loyal to Belimarius, is loyal only to itself.

Meitremar's cult infiltrated and took over Stonehome in Iron Harbor, subverting the local Gorum worshippers, before they were soundly defeated. Meitremar's soul has been taken by the leigons of hell.
