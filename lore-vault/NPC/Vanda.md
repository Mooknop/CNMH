Vanda was one of the acolytes of Stonehome, the garrison-church of Gorum in Iron Harbor, who fell under the influence of Meitremar's cult of Xar-Azmak when the Iron Harbor was infiltrated. Unlike the others who fully converted — trading their unrusted steel for the rusted armor and sickles that mark devotion to the dead demon lord — Vanda never made that final surrender.

In the wake of the crisis, Vanda stepped up to lead Stonehome, taking on the responsibilities of a temple left without clear direction after the corruption was purged. She has since thrown herself into rebuilding both the church and the community's trust in it.

She is currently in a situationship with Ashka B'gosh.
