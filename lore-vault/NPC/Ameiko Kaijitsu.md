Born Ameiko Kaijitsu, her true surname is Amatatsu — the last surviving rightful heir to the throne of Minkai. Before her ascension, she was best known in Sandpoint as the proprietor of the Rusty Dragon, the most popular inn and tavern in town, and as a former adventurer.

In 4707 AR, Ameiko lost both her father and half-brother during the goblin raids tied to the rise of Runelord Karzoug. In 4711 AR, she and a group of adventurers discovered her true heritage and embarked on a journey across the Crown of the World to Minkai, where she defeated the usurper Jade Regent and claimed the Jade Throne.

As of 4724 AR, Amatatsu Ameiko is Empress of Minkai. She still returns to Sandpoint on occasion for visits.
