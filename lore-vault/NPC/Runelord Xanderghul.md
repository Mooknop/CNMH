Runelord Xanderghul was the sole holder of the title of Runelord of Pride throughout all of Ancient Thassilon's history — there was only ever one. As the Runelord of Pride, he specialized in illusion magic, whose rune is the Crescent Scepter, and his sin's energy is violet.

Whether Xanderghul survived Earthfall in magical stasis, as some of his peers did, remains unknown.

Perhaps he was too prideful to run.
