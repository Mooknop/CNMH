Kaleb Valdemar wears the family name well in public, but his dealings run darker: his name appears among Aliver Podiker's incriminating ledgers, tying him to the organization called the Midnight Dawn.

By the time anyone thinks to look closely, Kaleb is gone — he scrubs his manor of evidence, hires mercenaries, and heads for the Pit. Asking around town only confirms that nobody has seen him since.
