Streaks of blue shimmer within the smoky depths of this perfect crystalline sphere. Both the scintillating shade and emanations of an indefinable power suggest that something slumbers within. When all is quiet, Blu can feel it pulling at him.

Blu found the orb before the events at Rusthenge, drawn toward some Ancient Thassilonian ruins where a patrol from Xin-Edasseril was cleaning out a trove of magical artifacts — the orb among them. What it was doing there, and why Xin-Edasseril's forces wanted it, remains unknown.

Jade determined it could be Activated, but attempting to do so backfired and discharged a bolt of electricity. The orb's full capabilities are not yet understood. It feels, in some hard-to-define way, awake — sometimes, when Blu holds it in quiet moments, he gets the distinct sense of being watched.

Given Blu's draconic lineage and his unusual connection to blue dragons specifically, the pull he feels toward the orb is unlikely to be coincidence.
