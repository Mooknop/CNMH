At the height of the Ancient Thassilonian Empire, Emperor Xin crafted seven Alara'quin: magical weapons of extraordinary power, each bound to one of his seven Runelords. Xanderghul's Flawless Hammer was the symbol of office for Xanderghul, the sole and eternal Runelord of Pride.

Xanderghul's domain was illusion magic, and the Hammer reflects this — it is not merely a weapon but a psychic instrument, attuned to pride and the manipulation of emotion. Its most notable property is its reaction to emotional effects: when the wielder would be adversely affected by an emotion, the Hammer attempts to counteract it with a surge of intense pride.

The weapon is currently carried by Jade Inferno, who recovered it and has integrated it into both their spellcasting and their ongoing investigation into New Thassilon's ambitions. Given that Xanderghul himself may have survived Earthfall — no body was ever confirmed — the Hammer's provenance is a subject Jade keeps in mind.

As an Alara'quin, it is an artifact-class item. It cannot be destroyed by conventional means.
