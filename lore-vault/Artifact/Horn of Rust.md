This rusted, coiled iron horn is sizable, appearing similar in shape to a ram's horn but measuring nearly two feet in diameter. Flakes of rust constantly fall from it, and it leaves hands with a rusty red stain that lingers.

The Horn of Rust was an instrument of Xar-Azmak, the demon lord of rust and corruption whose Abyssal realm of Vlorus borders the Plane of Metal. Though Xar-Azmak was destroyed by Dispater's armies long ago, his power persists in the Horn. Meitremar's cult made use of it in their rituals at Rusthenge, attempting to channel its remaining charge toward Xar-Azmak's resurrection.

The Horn cannot be destroyed by conventional means — as an artifact, it must be unmade through some other method. Ferrumnestra has personally asked the party to find a way to destroy it, viewing its continued existence as an open wound in her domain.

While the Horn's power has diminished over the ages since Xar-Azmak's death, those who carry it are still subject to its palpable chaotic influence. Creatures not sanctified Unholy are Enfeebled 1 while carrying or using it; those sanctified Holy are Enfeebled 2 and Stupefied 1. Worshippers of Xar-Azmak are immune to these penalties.
