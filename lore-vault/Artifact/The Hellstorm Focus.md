Deep in the catacombs beneath Sandpoint's cathedral stand slabs of ancient stone dense with Thassilonian runes, infused with magic that has outlasted empires. The device — if device it is — is connected to something the catacombs' ancient keepers called the Hellstorm Flume; a demon bound to the deep shrine has prayed for its reactivation since before Sandpoint existed.

Neither on-site study nor Brodert Quink's library can unravel it completely. The fullest surviving notes on the Focus lie elsewhere, in hands that have been studying it far more recently.
