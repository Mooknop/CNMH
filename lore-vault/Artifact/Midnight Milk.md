id: midnight-milk
summary: A pale, viscous substance rising up from the Darklands — those who drink it dream vividly and strangely, and some keep drinking.
---

A pale, viscous substance that has begun appearing in the deeper markets of Sekamina and, more recently, filtering up toward the surface. It has no taste. It barely has a smell — just something faintly mineral, faintly wrong.

Those who drink it sleep. And while they sleep, they dream.

The dreams are vivid beyond natural dreaming — coherent, persistent, strange. Dreamers report the same recurring imagery: vast dark spaces, slow movement through tunnels, a distant sound like breathing. Some wake disturbed but unharmed. A few return to it. A smaller number keep returning until they can't stop.

No one with any credibility will confirm where it comes from, only that it originates somewhere in the Darklands — almost certainly Orv, given the routes it travels. Who produces it, what it actually is, and what extended use does to a person remain unanswered questions.

It is not widely available. Yet.
