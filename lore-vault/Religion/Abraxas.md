Abraxas is the demon lord of forbidden magic and dangerous secrets, patron of those who believe no knowledge should stay hidden no matter the cost of knowing it. His cults have always been small, scholarly, and catastrophic.

In the age of Thassilon, a sect in his service kept the catacombs that lie beneath what is now Sandpoint's cathedral. A small marble shrine to the demon lord still burns there, deep below the town, tended longer than anyone would credit.
