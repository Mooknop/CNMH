Ashava is an empyreal lord — a celestial demigoddess — whose care is for spirits that have lost their way: the unmourned dead, souls too burdened or too bitter to pass on. Her faithful dance under the open night sky, and her rites are worked in moonlight, which her doctrine holds is the kindest light to call a spirit into.

Her worshippers are few but devoted healers and guides, and her faith holds that no spirit is beyond reaching — only beyond the patience of those who stopped trying.
