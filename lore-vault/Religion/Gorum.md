Gorum is the god of battle, strength, and valor — known to his faithful as Our Lord in Iron. He is a deity of pure martial virtue, caring not for law or chaos, good or evil, only for the glory found in worthy combat. His followers believe that the greatest honor a warrior can achieve is to die in battle, and they seek out worthy conflicts with fervor.

Gorum has a herald known as the First Blade. His followers in the Iron Archipelago's Iron Harbor have made the fortress-church of Stonehome the center of their devotion — though that institution has recently been compromised by the cult of Xar-Azmak.

Gorum is viewed as disinterested in inventors, even of weapons, and has come into conflict with Urgathoa, who considers death by disease insufficiently honorable for warriors.
