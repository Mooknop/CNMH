Dahak is the evil dragon deity of destruction, treachery, and wanton violence. He is the eternal enemy of Apsu, the good dragon god, and their conflict represents the cosmic division within dragonkind between the metallic (good) and chromatic (evil) lineages.

Dahak's portfolio encompasses senseless destruction, betrayal, and the pleasure taken in causing pain and ruin. He is worshipped primarily by chromatic dragons and those mortals who seek power through destruction. His connection to Blue Dragons — whose lightning and proud cruelty align with his domain — is part of why Blu-Kakke's discovered connection to Blue Dragon power scandalized the Brotherhood of the Platinum Band, which is devoted to Apsu.
