Apsu is the patron deity of all good and metallic dragons, and one of the oldest gods of the Great Beyond. According to the draconic creation myth, he was one of the primordial dragons who created the solar system and its inhabitants from the chaos of the Universe, making him a figure of cosmic significance in draconic religion.

Apsu is worshipped by the humanoid monastic order known as the Brotherhood of the Platinum Band, who venerate him as the source of righteous draconic power. His faith is small but sincere, and his followers tend toward the ideals of creation, guardianship, and the patient wisdom of ancient things.

Apsu's eternal enemy is Dahak, the evil dragon deity of destruction, treachery, and wanton violence.
