Asmodeus is the most powerful of the nine archdevils that inhabit Hell, and the only one of Hell's rulers to claim full divinity. He is credited with penning the Contract of Creation, within which his followers believe is hidden the means for their patron's eventual rise to supremacy over all other gods.

Other gods find Asmodeus the easiest of the evil deities to deal with — they recognize his aid is not to be sought lightly, but he operates within predictable frameworks of law and contract. He is allied with Abadar due to their shared appreciation for order and lawful societies. He has an ongoing feud with Irori, who dislikes him for undermining others' hard work. He holds a great disdain for Grandmother Spider, who stole his keys and outwitted his retribution.

Asmodeus serves as the patron deity of Cheliax, whose ruling houses bound themselves to infernal power after the chaos following Aroden's death.
