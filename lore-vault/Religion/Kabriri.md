Kabriri is the demon lord of ghouls and graveyards, worshipped by those who feed on the dead — and by the dead who feed. His faithful haunt tombs and warrens beneath the earth, and his rites are scrawled in blood over stolen bones.

The goblin ghouls who tunneled into the catacombs beneath Sandpoint's Boneyard raised just such a shrine: a flat-topped boulder smeared with his runes, on which they laid Nualia's stolen skeleton as an offering to prove their worth.
