Desna is the goddess of dreams, travelers, and freedom. She is one of the most widely worshipped deities in Varisia, particularly among the Varisian people who see her as a patron of the road and of personal liberty. Her faith emphasizes joy, exploration, and the beauty of the night sky.

The inclusion of a Desnan temple in the Sandpoint Cathedral was a condition of the compromise reached with the Varisian Council after the Scarnetti founding raid — a gesture of respect for Varisian nomadic culture. Father Abstalar Zantus, while a devoted cleric of Desna, keeps the cathedral open to all faiths.
