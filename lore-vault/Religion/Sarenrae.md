Known to her faithful as the Dawnflower, the Healing Flame, and the Everlight, Sarenrae is a goddess who teaches temperance and patience in all things. Compassion and peace are her greatest virtues, and if enemies of the faith can be redeemed, they should be — though she is not without wrath for those who refuse redemption and continue to cause harm.

Sarenrae is one of the deities whose faith is welcomed at the multi-faith Sandpoint Cathedral. Her worship is widespread throughout the Inner Sea region. Her enemies include the undead goddess Urgathoa, whose portfolio of excess and disease runs counter to everything Sarenrae values.

She is one third of the divine relationship known as the Prismatic Ray, alongside Desna and Shelyn.
