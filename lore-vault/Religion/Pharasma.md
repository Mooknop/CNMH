Pharasma is the goddess of birth, death, fate, and prophecy. Every mortal soul passes after death to her Boneyard — the vast plane of judgment from which Sandpoint's own cemetery takes its name — where it awaits her verdict and passage to its eternal reward or punishment. Her faith abhors undeath as a perversion of the natural cycle.

Her clergy preside over funerals and tend graveyards across Golarion, and her rites are woven into the Sandpoint Cathedral's care for the town's dead. A soul that dies with its affairs unsettled may wait long in the Boneyard's queue — and some, it is said, are too burdened by what they left behind to face judgment at all.
