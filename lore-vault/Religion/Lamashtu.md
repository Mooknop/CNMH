Lamashtu is the demon queen who clawed her way to true godhood, worshipped as the mother and maker of monsters. Her faith celebrates deformity, madness, and the fecund horror of the wild places; goblins, gnolls, and worse creatures across Varisia raise crude idols in her name.

Sandpoint knows her mark too well. It was Lamashtu to whom Nualia turned in her despair, and Lamashtu's blessing that twisted the woman's arm into a demonic talon before the heroes of seventeen years ago cut her down.
