Shelyn is the goddess of beauty, art, love, and music. She was born the daughter of the spirit-wolf Thron and shares her father with her half-brother Dou-Bral — who, after a long absence and mysterious corruption, returned as the god of pain and shadow, Zon-Kuthon. Shelyn still grieves this transformation and holds love for what her brother once was, even as she opposes what he has become.

Shelyn's faith celebrates creativity and joy in all its forms. She is one third of the divine relationship known as the Prismatic Ray alongside Desna and Sarenrae. Her worshippers are found wherever art is made, and her faith is welcomed at the Sandpoint Cathedral. She is closest among the gods to Brigh, goddess of invention, despite their apparent differences.
