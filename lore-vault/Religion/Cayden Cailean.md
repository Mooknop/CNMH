Cayden Cailean is the god of drink, partying, freedom, and adventure. He is one of three mortals known to have passed the Test of the Starstone — a near-impossible divine trial — and ascended to godhood, though legend holds he was drunk at the time and can no longer clearly remember why he agreed to attempt it.

His faith is beloved by free spirits, travelers, fighters who prefer brawling to strategy, and anyone who believes that life is best lived without unnecessary chains. He has no patience for tyranny or the oppression of the weak. His followers in Osprey Cove, like Elder Ordwi, embody the town's laid-back, community-minded values.

Cayden Cailean is on good terms with Brigh (who appreciates the science of brewing) and counts Desna and Shelyn among his friendly divine neighbors.
