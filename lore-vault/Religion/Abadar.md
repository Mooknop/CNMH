Abadar is the god of cities, law, merchants, and wealth, known to be a patient deity who maintains a strong neutral stance in his actions. He sets forth to expand civilization and order among the peoples of Golarion, believing that structured society and lawful trade are the foundations of prosperity and peace.

Abadar is allied with Asmodeus due to their shared appreciation for order and lawful societies, though the two have very different ends in mind. He has a good relationship with Irori. His faith is widespread in every major city of the Inner Sea and is welcomed at the Sandpoint Cathedral. His retired paladin Jasper Korvaski serves as Sandpoint's town planner — a man whose near-incorruptible character the Mercantile League values highly.
