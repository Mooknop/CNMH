Dispater is Hell's greatest jailer and politician, ruling its second layer, Dis. Known as the Iron Lord, Father of Dis, and First King, he is one of the most active rulers of Hell and one of Asmodeus's closest and longest-standing allies. He appears as a majestic, towering figure with rust-red skin, four horns, and a burning crown that hovers above his head — a fallen angel whose black wings he keeps retracted within his body at almost all times.

Dispater is a master strategist and expert in intrigue. He prefers to use his commanding presence, charm, and political leverage to achieve his ends rather than direct violence — but when violence becomes necessary, he reminds everyone precisely why he is feared. His symbol of office is an artifact known as the Eclipsing Eye. His religious symbol is an iron spike driven into a golden ring with a red and purple crown.

Alone among the lords of Hell, Dispater believes in the virtues of courtly love. He has taken three wives over his eons-long existence: the first is forgotten even by himself, her only memorial a shattered statue in his throne room. His second was Feronia, an elemental demigoddess, with whom he had a brief tempestuous relationship that produced the empyreal lord Ragathiel. His third and current wife is Erecura, a once-mortal demigoddess condemned to Hell for stealing the secret of eternal life from Pharasma — and whom Dispater sincerely loves.

Dispater's armies were responsible for the destruction of the demon lord Xar-Azmak, whose Abyssal realm Vlorus now lies unclaimed.
