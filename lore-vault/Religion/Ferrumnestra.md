Ferrumnestra is the Elemental Lord of Metal, known as the Lady of Rust. She is one of the two Elemental Lords of the Plane of Metal, her domain encompassing metal, decay, mourning, and the inevitability of endings. Her touch is that of slow decline — the rust of ages that eats away at even the strongest steel. While she has no particular desire to hasten any endings, she is there to consume the last crumbling detritus of what has fallen, leaving nonexistence in her wake.

Ferrumnestra keeps no permanent dwelling, always on the move as is her purpose. Her few devotees usually follow her example, traveling across the world and destroying the wreckage of ancient ruins in their wake. Her edicts are to accept the inevitable, clear what remains of that which has fallen to ruin, and usher the past into obscurity. Her anathema: to preserve knowledge that has outlived its time, or to restore an object or structure that has been reclaimed by the elements.

She is strongly opposed to the resurrection of Xar-Azmak, the dead demon lord whose realm Vlorus borders the Plane of Metal — viewing any attempt to restore him as a direct disrespect to her domain. She has chosen Pellias Scarnetti as her champion, a bond that his companion Jade Inferno has taken great interest in helping him explore.
