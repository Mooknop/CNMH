Pazuzu is the demon lord of the sky and of temptation, lord of the Abyssal realm of High M'vania and ancient, bitter enemy of Lamashtu. Where other demon lords conquer, Pazuzu corrupts: his favorite work is the slow ruin of a mortal soul, begun with something as small as speaking his name aloud.

His influence over the Lost Coast is older than Sandpoint itself — an ancient shrine to the winged king lies hidden in the rock of Chopper's Isle, and his servants have shaped more of the town's tragedies than anyone living suspects.
