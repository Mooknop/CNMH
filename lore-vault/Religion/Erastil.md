Erastil is one of the oldest human gods still worshipped in the Inner Sea region, his religion dating back to before the Age of Darkness, when small farming communities and hunter-gatherers prayed to him for bountiful harvests and successful hunts. He leads his followers by example and good deeds rather than flowery rhetoric.

Erastil believes that groups work best when led by a good and strong leader, and he values family, community, children honoring their parents, and understanding the difference between work and play. His faith is humble, practical, and deeply rooted in the rhythms of rural life.

His faith is welcomed at the multi-faith Sandpoint Cathedral and provides aid to the poor in Magnimar's rougher districts.
