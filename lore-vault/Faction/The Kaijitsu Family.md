The Kaijitsu family traveled across the Crown of the World from Minkai generations ago, eventually settling in Magnimar before becoming one of Sandpoint's founding families as glassmakers and jewelers. The family's profile in Magnimar declined after the death of the family head in a storm in 4687 AR.

Lonjiku Kaijitsu, the family's Sandpoint patriarch, was known for his sharp temper and difficult relationships with his children. His daughter Ameiko became an adventurer and innkeeper, defying all expectations — and eventually ascended to become Empress of Minkai. Lonjiku met a grisly end in the Sandpoint Glassworks during the 4707 AR goblin raids. With Ameiko's departure, the Kaijitsu family has effectively ended its presence in Sandpoint.
