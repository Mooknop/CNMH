The Valdemar family was once one of the most prosperous of Sandpoint's founding families, generating significant revenue through fishing, shipping, and logging. Found primarily in Magnimar and Sandpoint, the family's fortunes have declined sharply due to competition from the Scarnettis and a series of tragedies.

Ethram Valdemar, the family's Sandpoint patriarch, recently died. The Magnimar branch of the family has shown no interest in maintaining an active presence in Sandpoint, leaving their traditional council seat vacant and their businesses understaffed.
