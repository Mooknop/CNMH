The Deverin family is one of the most down-to-earth aristocratic families in Magnimar. Along with their modest town manor in the Naos district of Magnimar, they also own one of Magnimar's most successful breweries. A branch of the family sailed north in 4666 AR to found Sandpoint, and continues to live there today.

The current leader of the Sandpoint branch also serves as the town's mayor. Gaven Deverin, a cousin of the mayor, is the last surviving owner of Two Knight Brewing in Sandpoint.
