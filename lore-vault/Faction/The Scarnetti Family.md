The Scarnetti family controls Sandpoint's mills and lumber industry, making them indispensable to the town's economy despite being widely disliked. Many of Sandpoint's elderly Varisian locals still have not forgotten their part in the tragedy surrounding the town's founding.

The family is headed by Titus Scarnetti and maintains the most conservative, old-school Chelaxian values of any family in town. Recent years have been turbulent: rumors of collusion with local criminals after the Scarnetti Manor was damaged in a giant raid, and Titus allegedly secretly working with Korvosan interests to rebuild the family's fortunes has earned the suspicions of many locals.
