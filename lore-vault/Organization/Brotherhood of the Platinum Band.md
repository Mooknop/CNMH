The Brotherhood of the Platinum Band is a monastic order of humanoid worshippers devoted to Apsu the Waybringer, the primordial dragon god of creation and patron of metallic dragons. According to the draconic creation myth Apsu is one of the primordial dragons who created the solar system and its inhabitants from the chaos of the Universe. The Brotherhood reveres him as the source of all righteous draconic power.

The small faith operates mostly out of Oppara and Absalom. They are an unusual congregation in that they are composed of humanoids rather than dragons, drawn to Apsu's ideals of good, creativity, and the long arc of cosmic order.

The Brotherhood raised Blu-Kakke after he was left in their care as a child by 'a man made of metal' from the north east. He trained with them until a connection to Blue Dragons — chromatic dragons associated with evil in orthodox draconic religion — scandalized the Brotherhood and led to his expulsion on religious grounds.
