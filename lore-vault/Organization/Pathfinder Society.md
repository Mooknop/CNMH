The Pathfinder Society is a globe-spanning organization based out of Absalom, the City at the Center of the World. Its members — called Pathfinders — travel throughout Golarion to explore, delve, and document the lesser-seen parts of the world, sending journals back to their venture-captains. The most illuminating of these journals are compiled into the ongoing Pathfinder Chronicles series.

In Varisia, the Society operates out of Heidmarch Manor in Magnimar, run by Sheila and Canayven Heidmarch. They maintain ties with local scouts and take an active interest in Thassilonian ruins throughout the region.
