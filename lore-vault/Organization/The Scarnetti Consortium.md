The Scarnetti Consortium is a collection of industrial companies and concerns centered on Sandpoint's lumber and milling industries, all owned and directed by Titus Scarnetti, the head of the Scarnetti family. The Consortium controls the lumber supply that competing interests — especially the now-weakened Valdemars — depend on, and Titus leverages this dependency openly and without apology.

The Consortium has a deeply negative reputation with much of Sandpoint's population, particularly the Varisian community. Evidence of collusion with local criminal elements nearly surfaced publicly when Scarnetti Manor was damaged in a giant raid; Titus managed to suppress most of it, but enough leaked to cause lasting damage to the family's standing. Since then, the Consortium has been rebuilt through secret dealings with Korvosan interests.

The Consortium is distinct from the broader Sandpoint Mercantile League, which it participates in but frequently works against. Its influence rating in town is sharply negative.
