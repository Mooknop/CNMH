Little is known of the Devil's Disciples in town, which suits them. They venerate the Sandpoint Devil and have established themselves in the Pit, and their arrival was catastrophic for the goblin clans that previously held its upper levels.

They are not merely hermits in a hole: the cult's agents are bold enough to operate inside Sandpoint itself when their interests demand it.
