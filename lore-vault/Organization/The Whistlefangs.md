Goblin clans have squatted in the Pit's upper reaches for centuries, and the Whistlefangs are the latest — and nearly the last of their line. Two years ago they held everything above the fog; a soured alliance with two bickering caligni bands and the disastrous arrival of the Devil's Disciples has since reduced them to a fraction of their number, squeezed into the uppermost level against infestations of gremlins.

They were ready to abandon the Pit entirely when a miracle arrived: an undead figure in fiery armor calling itself Ripnugget. The clan's signature is the whistling speech they pipe through drilled teeth — not a true language, but a code of signals they use to coordinate ambushes and defense throughout their warren.
