Nisk Tander's shop sells alchemical items and potions in impressive variety, piled on shelves in a system only Nisk understands. His prices are fair and his enthusiasm genuine; his quality control is another matter, and locals know that the occasional bottle from his shelves simply doesn't work. Those who want surer goods pay more at Pillbug's Pantry — and weigh that against the rumors about its owner.
