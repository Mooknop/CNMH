Varisia has no central government. Its independent city-states — Magnimar, Korvosa, and the pirate haven of Riddleport — each hold sway over a small collection of towns and villages. The wilds beyond, including most of the Storval Plateau, are claimed by the Shoanti people. The neutral city of Kaer Maga is often discounted from political calculations altogether, and its inhabitants prefer it that way.

The human population is split between Varisians/Shoanti — tribes descended from Thassilon's enslaved workers who developed nomadic cultures of their own — and Chelaxian-descended settlers from the south. 

Ancient Thassilonian ruins are scattered throughout Varisia, relics of an empire destroyed by the cataclysm known as Earthfall ten thousand years ago. These ruins have taken on new significance with the recent return of New Thassilon and The Runelords.
