Turandarok Academy serves as both school and orphanage, funded partly by the town and partly by the personal fortune of its headmaster, the retired adventurer Ilsoari Gandethus. Ilsoari teaches, manages the orphans, and maintains a locked basement full of exotic weapons, strange maps, and monster trophies that he's happy to show adults who ask. The children spin elaborate theories about what's down there. Most of them are wrong. One of them is startlingly close.
