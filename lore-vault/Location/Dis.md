Dis is the second layer of Hell, and its name belongs both to the layer itself and to the immense iron city that fills it — a metropolis with a population of millions, a sprawling monument to what civilization becomes when order serves evil. It is one of the largest prisons in all the cosmos, and its ruler Dispater is its unyielding, merciless warden.

In contrast to the iron, fire, and stone that dominates most of Dis, a region called Eleusys — the personal palace of Dispater's wife Erecura — is filled with lush vegetation, a startling oasis of green within the infernal city. Erecura keeps her court there, attended by three norns who serve as her advisors and handmaidens.
