Hell, sometimes called the Pit, is the ultimate expression of evil order in the Great Beyond. It is a plane where the weak are subjugated to serve the strong, where complete obedience and unquestioning faith are the supreme virtues. Its tortures are not the willful, random cruelty of the Abyss, nor the purely sadistic spite of Abaddon — they are precise, purposeful, and honed to a razor's edge in service of a single goal: bending the will of souls and the very architecture of creation to the glory of the lord Asmodeus and his perfect order.

Hell is home to myriads of devils and other evil outsiders, and is the final destination for countless mortal souls who made infernal bargains in life. Its architecture is one of endless iron, fire, and stone, shaped into cities, prisons, and fortresses of terrible grandeur.

Hell is divided into nine layers, each ruled by an archdevil. Asmodeus rules from the deepest layer, Nessus. The second layer is Dis, an iron city-realm ruled by the archdevil Dispater.
