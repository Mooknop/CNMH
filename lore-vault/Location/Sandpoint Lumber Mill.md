The Sandpoint Lumber Mill is part of the Scarnetti family's commercial empire, operated under the thumb of its beleaguered manager Ibor Thorn. The mill functions, but Ibor has been suffering increasingly severe nightmares centered on the machinery, and his behavior has become erratic.
