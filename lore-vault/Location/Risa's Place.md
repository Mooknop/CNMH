Risa's Place has been a Sandpoint fixture since the town's founding, built on the warmth and strange tales of its founder Risa Magravi, a Varisian sorcerer who spent her nights traveling the Dreamlands in her sleep. She's left day-to-day operations to her three children, Besk, Lanalee, and Vodger, but remains a fixture of the taproom. Lately she's been silent and listless — she hasn't been able to dream, and her appearances have become shorter and less frequent.
