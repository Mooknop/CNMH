Cracktooth's is owned by Jesk 'Cracktooth' Berinni, who looks like a brawler and reads like a scholar. Nights when Jesk takes the stage to deliver his observations on Magnimar's politics are standing room only. The jagged teeth and thickset neck are somewhat misleading.
