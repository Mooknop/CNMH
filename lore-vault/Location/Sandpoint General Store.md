Sandpoint's general store is owned by Ven Vinder, who once took great pride in his business and his family. He's been spending most of his time in a cellar drinking hole since the murder of his eldest daughter. His wife Solsta runs the actual store and is increasingly worried about both her husband and their surviving daughter Shayliss, who has been coming home less and less.
