Madame Mvashti is one of Sandpoint's oldest residents, a Varisian seer whose reputation for genuine insight has outlasted the skepticism of every newcomer who dismissed her. She offers occult spellcasting and maintains a spare room for travelers, but those who come looking for a novelty fortune-teller tend to leave unsettled in ways they didn't anticipate.
