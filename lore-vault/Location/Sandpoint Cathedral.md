Sandpoint Cathedral is the town's spiritual center and one of its most prominent buildings. It was rebuilt after the 4702 AR fire that destroyed the original chapel and killed Father Ezakien Tobyn — an event still referred to locally as part of 'the Late Unpleasantness.' The new cathedral honors six deities: Abadar, Desna, Erastil, Gozreh, Sarenrae, and Shelyn, reflecting the diverse beliefs of Sandpoint's population. The Desnan shrine was a condition of the town's founding agreement with the Varisian Council.

Father Abstalar Zantus serves as high priest. Though personally a cleric of Desna, he ministers to followers of all faiths with genuine warmth. The cathedral offers healing magic, religious items, and divine scrolls, and serves as one of the town's two sources of divine spellcasting. The Sandpoint Boneyard is attached to the grounds.
