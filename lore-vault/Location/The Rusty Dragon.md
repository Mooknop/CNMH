The Rusty Dragon is the beating heart of Sandpoint's social life: the best food in town, a reputation for welcoming adventurers, and a history that includes its former proprietor Ameiko Kaijitsu ascending to become Empress of Minkai. The inn is now managed by Bethana Corwin, a halfling who handles the quieter side of the business, and Daviren Hosk, who handles security and heavy labor while nursing a profound and personal hatred of goblins.
