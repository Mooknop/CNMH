The Way North sells local and regional maps, novelty treasure maps, and the supplies to make your own, all under the proprietorship of Veznutt Parooh — a gnome cartographer and wizard of modest but practical talent who also sells arcane spellcasting services. Veznutt is forever hungry for new maps of anywhere, and pays for good ones.
