Junker's Edge is the waste disposal point for most of Sandpoint's refuse, a stretch of cliff from which garbage is dumped into the sea below. It is overseen by Gorvi, the town's half-orc dung-sweeper, who employs a small crew of youths and lives in a shack on Tower Street by choice rather than necessity.
