Magnimar stands as one of the two major city-states of southern Varisia, sitting in the shadow of the most awe-inspiring ruins of Ancient Thassilon: the crumbled cyclopean bridge called the Irespan. The city is ambitious and cosmopolitan, governed by a Lord-Mayor and the Council of Ushers, with a third, less official body — the Varisian Council — representing the nomadic Varisian people.

Sandpoint is technically a holding of Magnimar, though its isolated location means the city's direct authority is limited. The four founding families of Sandpoint — the Deverins, Scarnettis, Kaijitsus, and Valdemars — all trace their origins to Magnimar's aristocracy.

Magnimar is divided into numerous districts, from the wealthy Alabaster District to the criminal-ridden Underbridge slums. Criminal organizations such as the Night Scales and the Sczarni gangs operate in its shadows. The Pathfinder Society maintains a lodge here at Heidmarch Manor.
