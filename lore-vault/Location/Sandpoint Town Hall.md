Sandpoint Town Hall hosts the town's public meetings and houses offices for its councilors, with a secure vault belowground that doubles as the town's bank. The councilors all have businesses and duties of their own, so in practice the building belongs to its clerk: Shayliss Vinder, who knows where every record, key, and favor in Sandpoint's government is kept.
