The Sandpoint Boutique stocks a little of everything: equipment, clothing, weapons, tools, books, toys, and art objects, much of it sold on consignment or salvaged from abandoned places. Hayliss Korvaski runs it with brisk Abadaran rectitude and also sells divine spellcasting services — healing chief among them.
