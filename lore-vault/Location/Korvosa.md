Korvosa is the eldest of Varisia's three city-states, once part of Imperial Cheliax but now independent. The settlement originally served as a defensive position for settlers entering Varisia, growing over time into a cosmopolitan center of trade. Korvosa is built upon the ruins of the Ancient Thassilonian city of Xin-Eurythnia, once the capital of the realm ruled by Runelord Sorshen, the Runelord of Lust.

Korvosa maintains strong ties to Chelaxian tradition and culture, which puts it at odds with the more progressive Magnimar.
