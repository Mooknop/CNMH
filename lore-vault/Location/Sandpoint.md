Sandpoint sits in a natural cove along the cliff-lined Lost Coast of Varisia, roughly fifty miles northeast of Magnimar. It is Varisia's sixth-largest settlement and the only notable stop along this stretch of coast — a town that calls itself the Light of the Lost Coast and mostly believes it. Its reputation for arts, theater, and fine glasswork coexists uneasily with a history of serial murder, goblin raids, giant attacks, Runelord incursions, sinkholes into Thassilonian ruins, and tsunamis.

The town was founded in 4666 AR by four powerful families from Magnimar — the Kaijitsus, Valdemars, Scarnettis, and Deverins — who arrived to find Varisian travelers already using the land for their winter camps. The negotiations that followed went badly, though the resulting scandal was smoothed over, eventually, with a profit-sharing arrangement with the Varisian Council of Magnimar. That arrangement expires in 4726 AR. The town has not forgotten how it started.

Government sits nominally with Mayor Kendra Deverin, a capable diplomat whose personal style of governance has earned her genuine affection. The Sandpoint Town Council shares power with the Mercantile League, the Cathedral, and the increasingly fractious interests of the remaining founding families. The sheriff is Belor Hemlock, a Shoanti man who took the job under terrible circumstances and has kept it well.
