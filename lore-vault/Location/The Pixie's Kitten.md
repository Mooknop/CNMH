The Pixie's Kitten is Sandpoint's open secret — a brothel operated with a discretion that most of the town appreciates and none of the Scarnettis will acknowledge. It offers lodging as well as its primary services, and its proprietor, Kaye Tresarani, is a prominent enough figure in town that her establishment operates without serious harassment.
