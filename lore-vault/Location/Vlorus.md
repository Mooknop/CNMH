Vlorus is the Abyssal realm once ruled by the deceased demon lord Xar-Azmak before his death by Dispater's armies. It is now unclaimed by any power. The valley realm is full of rusted junk and buildings, some as large as the castles that circle the tops of the valley in which the realm is located.
