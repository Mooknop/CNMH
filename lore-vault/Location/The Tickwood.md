The Tickwood is a relatively small forested area in the Sandpoint Hinterlands, located on the northern banks of the Turandarok River. It is less than two miles in length, separated from the coastal Shank's Wood to its north by the Lost Coast Road. To its west lie the Ravenroost hills, while Leb's Tors rise to the east.

The Tickwood is a favorite spot for local hunters, as it is thought to be free of goblin tribes, and is home to many deer, wild boars, and the occasional firepelt cougar. Stories in Sandpoint greatly exaggerate the size of the giant ticks that also dwell here, keeping many cautious citizens off the forest paths.

At the heart of the wood stands Akoni's Grove, the court of the dryad Akoni, who presides over the Tickwood's fey inhabitants. A troop of redcaps has recently moved into the area and though they will not establish permanent roots without the dryad's permission, they seem confident they can obtain it one way or another.
