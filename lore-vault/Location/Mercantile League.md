The Mercantile League's town office is the clearinghouse for Sandpoint's overland and seaborne trade: it books caravan passage, dispatches messengers, arranges shipping, and brokers property sales. Its guildmaster, the retired Abadaran paladin Jasper Korvaski, runs the place with scrupulous honesty, and a party that earns his trust can pick up guild work — keeping the books, drafting contracts, and running the League's errands around town.
