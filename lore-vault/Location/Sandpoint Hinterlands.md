The Sandpoint Hinterlands is the name for the area surrounding the town of Sandpoint. It encompasses a wide variety of terrain including Brinestump Marsh to the west, the Nettlewood and Mosswood forests, the Tickwood, the rocky Devil's Platter escarpment, the mysterious Turandarok River, and several areas of open moor. Multiple goblin tribes make their homes in the hinterlands.

The Turandarok River is said to lead, eventually, to the First World — an extraordinary claim that has driven many expeditions to madness. Closer to town, the Tickwood offers relatively safe hunting grounds for locals — when one accounts for its fey inhabitants.
