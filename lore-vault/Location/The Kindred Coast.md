The Kindred Coast is the name for the coast of an island in the Iron Archipelago, a region that has come under the political umbrella of New Thassilon. It is home to the brother-towns of Osprey Cove and Iron Harbor, both founded by escaped slaves from the pirating hub of Riddleport who sought a new life free of the city's violence and exploitation.

The region is notable for its proximity to significant Ancient Thassilonian ruins, most notably Rusthenge. This has drawn the attention of Runelord Belimarius's government, which has begun extending its influence into the area through operatives like Meitremar.
