The Old Light is a ruined lighthouse that stretches above Sandpoint's coastal bluffs, a relic of the Thassilonian empire that predates the town by millennia. Most residents treat it as a landmark. Brodert Quink, the local Thassilonian scholar, has spent years arguing that it was never a lighthouse at all — that it was a war machine capable of projecting fire at range. Nobody believed him, for a long time. The return of New Thassilon and its Runelords has made people reconsider.
