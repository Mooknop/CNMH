The Sandpoint Glassworks was for decades one of the town's great economic engines, operated by the Kaijitsu family whose skill with glass was foundational to Sandpoint's cultural identity. After Ameiko Kaijitsu inherited the building and departed for Minkai, she closed the glassworks rather than deal with persistent rumors that it was haunted by victims of a prior tragedy. It stands empty now — the furnaces cold, the windows dark.
