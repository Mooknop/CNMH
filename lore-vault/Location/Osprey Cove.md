Osprey Cove is a small, community-minded town on the Kindred Coast of the Iron Archipelago, founded by escaped slaves from the pirate city of Riddleport. Its people have built a culture that prizes personal freedom, communal support, and a close relationship with the natural world. The town is generally full of worshippers of Cayden Cailean, and its druids and farmers set the tone for a settlement that is relaxed, egalitarian, and occasionally eccentric.

Osprey Cove is governed informally by a council of Elders, currently including Milton Scarnetti and Elder Ordwi — a cleric of Cayden Cailean and a known friend of the natural world. The town stands in marked contrast to its sister settlement Iron Harbor across the water: where Iron Harbor is gruff, martial, and devoted to Gorum, Osprey Cove is gentle, communal, and welcoming to outsiders.

The Kindred Coast region lies close to significant Ancient Thassilonian ruins, most notably Rusthenge, which has drawn the attention of Runelord Belimarius's government in Xin-Edasseril.
