Pillbug's Pantry operates as a perfectly respectable alchemy shop under the ownership of Aliver 'Pillbug' Podiker. It sells medicines and potions that the town genuinely needs. It also, for the right customers who know to ask the right question, sells poisons and other morally dubious alchemical concoctions.
