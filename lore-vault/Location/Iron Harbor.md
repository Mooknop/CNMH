Iron Harbor is the sister settlement to Osprey Cove, situated on the mainland beside the island that holds the Thassilonian ruin of Rusthenge. Where Osprey Cove is gentle and communal, Iron Harbor is gruff, militaristic, and fiercely devoted to Gorum, the Lord of Battle. The town's center of power is Stonehome, a structure that serves simultaneously as garrison and temple.

Recently, Stonehome and Iron Harbor have been infiltrated and partially taken over by followers of the dead demon lord Xar-Azmak, operating under the direction of Meitremar — a scholar and operative who serves Runelord Belimarius's government in Xin-Edasseril. The loyal Gorumites who survived the takeover of Stonehome were handed over to Meitremar's sinspawn ally Azomi.
