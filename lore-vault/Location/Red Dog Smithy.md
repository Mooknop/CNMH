Named for Das Korvut's beloved mastiffs, which lounge around the property at all times, the Red Dog Smithy produces excellent weapons and armor. Das has a foul temper, no patience for customers, and a habit of drunk midnight ranting that the town tolerates because the metalwork is genuinely superb. He's also illegally converted a vacant lot next door into a private cemetery for two mastiffs killed in the goblin raid, which has put the town planner in an awkward position.
