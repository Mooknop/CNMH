The Hagfish is a rowdy waterfront tavern popular with sailors, fishers, and people who enjoy a challenge. The house challenge involves drinking from a tank of slime produced by the bar's resident hagfish, Norah. Those who manage it have their names written on the ceiling. The proprietor, Jargie Quinn, has a different story every night about how he lost his leg, and the yarning is half the draw.
