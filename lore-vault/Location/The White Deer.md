The White Deer offers massive rooms for large parties, as well as the most luxurious accomodations. Its owner, Garridan Viskalai, keeps the place immaculate in an attempt to lure The Next Big Adventuring Party to get some of the spotlight that the Rusty Dragon enjoys. His gruff demeanor undercuts the effort somewhat, but the quality of the rooms speaks for itself. Garridan is the brother of Sheriff Belor Hemlock — a fact neither of them discusses without visible discomfort.
