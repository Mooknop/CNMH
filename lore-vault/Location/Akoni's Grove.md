Akoni's Grove occupies the deepest part of the Tickwood, where the oldest and largest redwood trees stand. Here the dryad Akoni presides as lord of the wood's fey inhabitants, holding court over a retinue of sprites and grigs.

A family of redcaps — led by their self-styled patriarch 'Grandfather' Grustmier — had moved into the Tickwood and begun quietly poisoning the great redwood tree to which Akoni is bound, weakening the dryad until he had no choice but to comply with their demands.

Now chased off, the Grove is returning to normalcy, though Akoni still worries about what brought the redcaps to his woods in the first place...
