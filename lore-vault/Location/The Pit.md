Every local has a story about the Pit, and most of them end badly. It is an open shaft in the heart of Devil's Platter, the rocky escarpment a couple of miles southeast of Sandpoint — perpetually fogged below its rim, ringed by jagged stone, and home (the stories insist) to the Sandpoint Devil itself.

Adventurers used to try their luck in its caves semi-regularly. Few came back, and these days most seek their fortunes elsewhere; the most recent company to descend was wiped out, leaving a single survivor. Those who want more than tavern tales are usually pointed at the sage Brodert Quink and his shelves of old books.
