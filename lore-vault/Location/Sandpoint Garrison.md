The Sandpoint Garrison houses the town watch and serves as the base of operations for Sheriff Belor Hemlock. It is a practical, solid structure befitting a town that has needed to defend itself more than once. Employment at the garrison is one of the more stable jobs in Sandpoint, and Belor runs a professional operation despite the budget constraints of a town this size.
