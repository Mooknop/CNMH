Cheliax is one of the most powerful nations of the Inner Sea region, a former empire whose reach once extended across much of western Avistan. After the death of the god Aroden, Cheliax fell into civil war and chaos, from which it emerged under the thumb of devil-worshipping aristocrats who bound themselves to the power of Hell. Today, Cheliax is ruled by an alliance of infernal-pact noble houses, with Asmodeus as the state's patron deity.

Chelaxian culture values order, hierarchy, law, and tradition — virtues taken to their darkest extreme in the service of Hell. The nation maintains the Hellknights, a collection of iron-disciplined knightly orders that enforce law with brutal efficiency, and its influence spreads through vassal states like Isger and former colonies like now-independent Vidrian.

In Sandpoint, Chelaxian cultural influence is most visible in the founding Scarnetti family, who cling to 'old values from Cheliax that are, in many cases, outdated.' The Chelaxian half of Sandpoint's founding population — settlers who came north via Magnimar — forms roughly half the town's human demographic.
