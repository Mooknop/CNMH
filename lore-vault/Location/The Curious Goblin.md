The Curious Goblin is a well-stocked bookshop whose prices reflect its proprietor's priorities — Chask Haladan values the books for their own sake, not for what they'll fetch. His adventuring youth gave him enough of a nest egg to run the shop on his own terms, which means most locals can't afford to shop there regularly. He also offers arcane spellcasting for those who ask.
