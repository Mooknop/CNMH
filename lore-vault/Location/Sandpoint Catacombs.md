When Nualia's grave was found torn open, the tunnel beneath it led somewhere no one expected: a complex of worked-stone chambers far older than Sandpoint, dating to the age of ancient Thassilon. Goblin ghouls had nested in the upper halls, raising a bloody shrine to Kabriri on which they bundled Nualia's stolen skeleton.

The deeper chambers hold older mysteries — fire-blasted rooms, a shrine where an ancient flame still burns, and great slabs of rune-carved stone whose purpose not even the town's sages can yet name.
