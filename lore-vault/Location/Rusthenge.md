Rusthenge is the most significant of the Ancient Thassilonian ruins near the Kindred Coast, situated on an island close to Iron Harbor. Like most Thassilonian ruins scattered across northwestern Avistan, it is a remnant of the empire that was destroyed by Earthfall ten thousand years ago.

The ruin has taken on new and sinister significance thanks to the scholar Meitremar, who has been using research found within Rusthenge to pursue the resurrection of the long-dead demon lord Xar-Azmak — the former ruler of the Abyssal realm of Vlorus. Meitremar operated under the political cover of Runelord Belimarius's government in Xin-Edasseril, lending the power of New Thassilon to his dangerous work.
