Sandpoint's graveyard adjoins the Cathedral grounds and is maintained by Naffer Vosk with meticulous care. It serves as both burial ground and a source of divine magic for those who know to ask. Quiet, orderly, and tended by a man who once lived on the wrong side of the law — it is, somehow, one of the more peaceful places in town.
