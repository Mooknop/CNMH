The Sandpoint Theater is the town's most celebrated cultural institution and a point of genuine civic pride. Its proprietor, Cyrdak Drokkus, built it into something that can hold its own against the playhouses of Magnimar — a fact he takes great pleasure in, given that he was forced to flee Magnimar in 4700 AR under circumstances he enjoys hinting at without ever quite explaining.

Cyrdak is married to Jasper Korvaski, a retired paladin of Abadar and town planner whose involvement in the Mercantile League has shielded both men from the Scarnettis' persistent disapproval of their marriage.
