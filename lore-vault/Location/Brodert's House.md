Brodert Quink's house is less a residence than a library with a bed in it. Sandpoint's foremost expert on Thassilonian lore — and self-appointed scholar of the Old Light — keeps decades of tomes, monographs, and half-finished theories here, and is always delighted by visitors who ask the right questions. Whether his advice is free depends on how much he likes you.
