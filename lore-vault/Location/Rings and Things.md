Maver Kesk's shop trades in jewelry and art objects, and provides Sandpoint's closest thing to formal moneylending. Every couple of months Maver organizes a guarded caravan south to Magnimar to make deposits at the Bank of Abadar — a regular bit of work for trustworthy blades.
