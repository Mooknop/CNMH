Kaijitsu Manor stands as a monument to a family that made Sandpoint's glasswork famous and then departed for a far grander stage. With Ameiko Kaijitsu now Empress of Minkai, the manor is maintained but quiet, and the glassworks it once supported stand closed and rumored-haunted nearby.
