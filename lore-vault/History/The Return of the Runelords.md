---

In 4718 AR, after awakening from her slumber, Runelord Alaznist employed the Scepter of Ages to travel back in time and replace the history of Golarion with one where she rules over a New Thassilon stretching from the Lands of the Linnorm Kings to Ustalav and Nidal and beyond. Her efforts were foiled by a group of adventurers working under her rival Sorshen. By the time Alaznist was defeated, most runelords were also dead, except Sorshen and Belimarius, who claimed the land that would become New Thassilon.
