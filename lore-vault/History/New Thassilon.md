New Thassilon is the returned civilization of Ancient Thassilonian peoples — survivors and time-displaced subjects of the old empire who have re-established a presence in Varisia. It is currently split between the rule of two Runelords: Runelord Belimarius (Runelord of Envy) and Runelord Sorshen (Runelord of Lust), who has taken a surprisingly different path from her former colleagues.

The return of New Thassilon has sparked renewed interest in Thassilonian history and ruins throughout Varisia. It has also created a new wave of worship for two long-lost gods: Lissala (goddess of runes, once the patron deity of Thassilon) and the Peacock Spirit (a mysterious god of success and prosperity).
