In 4666 AR, four powerful families from Magnimar consolidated their resources and sailed north to found Sandpoint. Each family brought a different industry: the Kaijitsus were glassmakers and jewelers, the Valdemars shipbuilders and carpenters, the Scarnettis loggers and millers, and the Deverins farmers and brewers.

Their founding was marred almost immediately. They arrived to find the land already inhabited by a large Varisian tribe. After some failed negotiations, a scandal leaving dozens of Varisians and Scarnettis dead rocked the negotiations. The resulting crisis threatened the establishement of the town all together until a compromise was reached: the Varisians received a sixty-year share of Sandpoint profits (later extended to 4726 AR) and the promise of a Desnan temple in the cathedral.

Today, the Deverins and Scarnettis remain Sandpoint's most influential founding families. The Kaijitsus departed when Ameiko Kaijitsu ascended to become Empress of Minkai. The Valdemars have effectively withdrawn from town politics following financial setbacks and the death of their Sandpoint patriarch.
