Referred to locally as 'the Late Unpleasantness,' a series of catastrophic events struck Sandpoint in the winter of 4702 AR. First, a string of gruesome murders was carried out by a killer known only as 'Chopper' took the lives of dozens of townspeople before being stopped during his last terrible night.

Shortly afterward, a great fire destroyed the original Sandpoint chapel and the surrounding buildings, killing Father Ezakien Tobyn. Tobyn was succeeded by Abstalar Zantus, who took on the task of rebuilding the cathedral and the faith of the people of Sandpoint
